# Declutr Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

Declutr is built on a zero-knowledge privacy architecture. This policy details how we collect, protect, and handle your data.

## 1. Zero-Knowledge Cryptography

Authentication credentials use SRP-6a Zero-Knowledge Proofs. Raw passwords and master decryption keys are never transmitted to or stored on Declutr servers.

## 2. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Declutr complies with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Users can request a complete export of personal data or invoke full account purge via `/api/v1/persona/export` and `/api/v1/memory/reset`.
